What is WorldCache?

Every heard of Geocaching? Geocaching is a outdoor, recreational treasure hunt, founded in 2000. Since then millions of caches are scatterted across the world, waiting for users to find them. WorldCache is a third party website that makes finding caches easier and more accesible for all.

What are the Primary Differences Between Geocaching.com and WorldCache?

WorldCache makes finding Geocaches even easier! With the reimagined, dynamic design, information is easier to find then ever! WorldCache also is a community for Geocaching. It allows you to meet other Geocachers and even find a cache or two together!
